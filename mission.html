Our Mission

Guided by our motto, "Virtute ad Veritatem" (Through Virtue to Truth), Philo Classical Academy is committed to cultivating virtuous scholars who seek truth through rigorous study and noble character. We aim to foster a deep appreciation for the Western intellectual tradition, encouraging students to pursue truth, goodness, and beauty in all aspects of their lives.

Our mission is to provide a classical education that equips students with the intellectual and moral tools to lead lives of purpose, wisdom, and service.
