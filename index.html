About Philo Classical Academy

History

Founded in 2010, Philo Classical Academy began as a small community of educators and families passionate about classical education. Over the years, we’ve grown into a leading great books school in the Twin Cities, committed to preserving the Western intellectual tradition.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to cultivate virtuous scholars who pursue truth, goodness, and beauty through a rigorous classical curriculum. We envision a future where our graduates lead lives of wisdom, integrity, and service, impacting their communities for the better.

Location

Located in the heart of the Twin Cities, Minnesota, our campus offers a serene environment for learning, surrounded by cultural and educational resources that enrich our students’ experiences.

Employment

We are always seeking passionate educators and staff who share our commitment to classical education. Visit our careers page to explore current openings and join our mission to shape the next generation of thinkers.

Faculty & Staff

Our dedicated faculty comprises experts in classical studies, literature, philosophy, and more. With a low student-to-teacher ratio, our staff provides personalized guidance, fostering intellectual and moral growth in every student.
