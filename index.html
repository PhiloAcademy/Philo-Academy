About Philo Classical Academy

Nestled in the heart of the Twin Cities, Philo Classical Academy is a K–12 school rooted in the great books tradition. We cultivate intellectual virtue, moral character, and aesthetic appreciation through a rigorous liberal arts curriculum.

Our students read primary texts, debate ideas, and pursue wisdom — preparing not just for college, but for a life well-lived.

Why Classical Education?

Classical education fosters wonder, virtue, and lifelong learning through the pursuit of truth, goodness, and beauty. Our approach builds character and critical thinking for a flourishing life.
