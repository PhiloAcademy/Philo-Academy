Welcome

At St. David’s, we believe God has a royal calling for every child: to grow in wisdom, stature, and the grace of God and man (Luke 2:52).

Our Mission

Provide an education rooted in Holy Scripture, the Western heritage, and the pursuit of excellence.

Our Vision

Raise learned disciples of Jesus who confess the faith with joy and serve with competence, humility, and virtue.

Four Guiding Lamps

Christ-centered

Christ is the lens through which all knowledge is understood and made complete.

Classical

The liberal arts form wisdom, eloquence, and love for what is good, true, and beautiful.

Lutheran

Doctrinal clarity forms students to stand firm on biblical truth in a wavering world.

Vocational

Parents, church, and civil order harmonize for whole-person formation.
