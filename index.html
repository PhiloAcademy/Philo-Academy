About Philo Classical Academy

Located in the vibrant Twin Cities, Philo Classical Academy is a classical, great books school dedicated to the pursuit of truth, goodness, and beauty. Our rigorous curriculum, grounded in the Western intellectual tradition, fosters nobility, virtue, and intellectual excellence. With a unique emphasis on traditional Western martial training, we cultivate both mind and body.
