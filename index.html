About Philo Academy

Located in the vibrant Twin Cities, Philo Academy is a classical, great books school dedicated to the pursuit of truth, goodness, and beauty. Our rigorous curriculum, grounded in the Western intellectual tradition, fosters nobility, virtue, and intellectual excellence. With a unique emphasis on traditional Western martial training, we cultivate both mind and body.

Our School

Philo Academy is a beacon of classical education, fostering a serious and academic environment where students engage with the great books and timeless ideas.

Academics

Our curriculum is rooted in the great books of the Western tradition, fostering deep engagement with timeless ideas. Students study classical languages, literature, philosophy, and mathematics, while our unique physical education program incorporates traditional Western martial training to build discipline and resilience.

We emphasize the appreciation of beauty in art, music, and literature, complementing our pursuit of truth and goodness. Explore our programs for different grade levels to learn more.
