6-9 Program

The 6-9 program at Philo Academy deepens students’ engagement with the great books, fostering critical thinking and a love for learning. Students study classic texts such as *The Odyssey* and *Aesop’s Fables*, discussing timeless themes in seminars led by our expert faculty.

Our curriculum includes advanced studies in Latin and Greek, alongside mathematics, history, and natural sciences. We introduce traditional Western martial training, teaching discipline and resilience through physical education classes that emphasize historical combat techniques.

Students also explore the arts, engaging with classical music, literature, and visual arts to appreciate beauty as a complement to truth and goodness. This holistic approach ensures a well-rounded education that prepares students for the challenges of high school and beyond.
