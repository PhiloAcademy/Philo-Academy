10-12 Program

The 10-12 program at Philo Classical Academy offers a rigorous classical education, preparing students for college and beyond through deep engagement with great books, advanced coursework, and a focus on virtue and wisdom.

Students study a comprehensive curriculum including literature, history, mathematics, science, Latin, philosophy, and the arts, culminating in a senior thesis that showcases their intellectual growth.
