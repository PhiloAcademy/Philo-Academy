Academics at Philo Classical Academy

At Philo Classical Academy, our classical great books curriculum fosters intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and moral virtue. Rooted in the Western intellectual tradition, our programs engage students with timeless texts and ideas, preparing them for a life of wisdom and excellence.

Lower School (K-5)

Our Lower School program introduces young learners to the great books through engaging stories, classical languages, and foundational skills in literature, math, science, and the arts, fostering a love of learning and virtuous character.

Learn More

Middle School (6-9)

The Middle School program deepens students’ engagement with the great books, emphasizing critical thinking, classical texts, and interdisciplinary studies in literature, history, math, science, and the arts to build intellectual rigor.

Learn More

Upper School (10-12)

Our Upper School program prepares students for higher education through advanced study of the great books, rigorous academics in literature, humanities, math, and science, and a focus on eloquent expression and moral leadership.
